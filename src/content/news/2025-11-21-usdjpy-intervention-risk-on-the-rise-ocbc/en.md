USD/JPY continues to hover near elevated levels, reflecting ongoing concerns over Japan's fiscal health, delays in Bank of Japan (BOJ) policy normalization, and the strength of the USD. The recent government approval of a substantial stimulus package amounting to JPY17.7 trillion, significantly higher than last year's JPY13.9 trillion, is adding to the pressure on the Japanese Yen.

As the USD maintains its strength, the potential for intervention in the currency pair is on the rise. Traders are closely monitoring these developments, as continued JPY weakness could lead to increased volatility in the exchange rate. The market remains vigilant, weighing the implications of Japan's fiscal measures against the backdrop of a resilient USD.
