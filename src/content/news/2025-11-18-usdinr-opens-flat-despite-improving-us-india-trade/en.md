The USD/INR currency pair opened flat on Tuesday, reflecting cautious market sentiment despite optimistic developments surrounding the US-India trade deal. The exchange rate showed little movement, as traders await further signals from economic indicators. The stability in the INR comes as both nations look to strengthen their trade ties, which could impact future forex trading dynamics.

Investors are closely monitoring potential advancements in negotiations, as any favorable outcomes could bolster the Indian Rupee against the US Dollar. Market analysts suggest that improvements in trade relations may provide the INR with an upward trajectory, although immediate trading remains subdued. The current exchange rate remains pivotal for traders as they gauge the potential for shifts in the currency pair amid evolving economic landscapes.
