EUR/USD remains steady around the 1.1540 mark, trading at 1.1533 on Thursday, following a robust U.S. jobs report that indicates a resilient labor market. This positive economic data surprised many traders and has reinforced bets on a dovish stance from the Federal Reserve as the economy shows signs of cooling in the latter half of 2025.

The exchange rate reflects market sentiment, with investors weighing the implications of the employment figures on future monetary policy. The solid labor statistics suggest that while the economy is still strong, the anticipated slowdown could keep the Fed from tightening its stance aggressively, impacting the trading dynamics between the euro and the dollar in the near term.
