The latest Reuters poll suggests a bullish outlook for the Australian dollar, contributing to the upward movement in the AUD/JPY exchange rate. Traders are closely monitoring these developments, as a sustained rise past the 102.00 level could signal further strength for the AUD against the yen, potentially impacting broader trading dynamics in the forex market.
