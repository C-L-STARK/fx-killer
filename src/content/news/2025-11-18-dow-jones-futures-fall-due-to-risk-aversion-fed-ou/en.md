Investors are closely monitoring economic signals that could influence the Fed's decisions, adding to market unease.

In the forex market, the prevailing sentiment is impacting key currency pairs, with the USD struggling against the EUR amid speculations about interest rate adjustments. The exchange rate dynamics are expected to fluctuate as traders respond to the evolving economic landscape, reflecting broader apprehensions in equity markets.
