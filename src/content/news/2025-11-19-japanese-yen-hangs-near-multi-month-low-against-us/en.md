The Japanese Yen (JPY) is trading in a narrow range during the Asian session on Wednesday, hovering near its multi-month low against the US Dollar (USD). Following a decline the previous day, the JPY remains under pressure, reflecting ongoing fiscal concerns and uncertainty regarding the Bank of Japan's (BoJ) monetary policy stance.

As the exchange rate fluctuates, market participants are closely watching the JPY's performance, particularly as it approaches levels not seen since late February. This trend suggests a cautious sentiment among traders, who are weighing potential implications for future trading strategies involving the USD/JPY currency pair amid prevailing economic challenges.
